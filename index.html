Interactive Video Player

Interactive video player using JavaScript, the HTML5 Video API, and MediaElement.js that synchronizes the video and transcript, and allows users to jump to specified parts of the video by clicking the transcript text. Additonally, the transcript is highlited as the matching section of the video plays. The video player is styled and customized with MediaElement.js and CSS
